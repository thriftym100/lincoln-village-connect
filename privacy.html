Guest Wi-Fi Privacy Notice

Last updated: July 20, 2026

Information collected

The portal may collect the email address you submit, your marketing preference, connection date and time, and basic technical information needed to operate and secure the guest network.

How information is used

Information is used to provide Wi-Fi access, maintain network security, measure portal usage, and, only when you opt in, send occasional promotions and coupons.

Marketing choice

Marketing consent is optional and is not required to receive guest Wi-Fi. Promotional emails will include an unsubscribe option.

Sharing

Information may be processed by service providers used to operate the portal, network, analytics, or email program. Information is not sold.

Contact

Privacy questions may be sent to Contact@thriftywashlincolnvillage.com.
